Conclusion

The socket infrastructure is a layered construction where each phase depends on the mathematical presence of the previous. Without the Slab and VFS caches (Phase I/II), the sock_init logic fails at the memory transaction layer. The enforcement of this sequence by the Linker ensures O(1) reliability across all instances of socket creation.
